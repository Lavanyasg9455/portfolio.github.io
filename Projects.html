Bone Cancer Detection Using CNN Algorithm

  • Description:The main aim is to find out the affected part i.e. uncontrolled growth and stage of disease in accurate manner by comparing medical imaging technique such as X-ray scan. The scanned images may not have high resolution due to the number of slices per pixel and noise, so as an initial step need to preprocess the images using median filter which is used to remove the noise in an image. The extracted images are classified using CNN classifier and stored, based on classification need to identify the stage of disease which is useful for the physician to give therapeutical suggestions. The proposed system result shows the improved rate of early detection of Bone cancer.
